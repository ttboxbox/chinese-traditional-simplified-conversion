About This Project關於本專案

Polyglot Converter was born from a simple idea: communication should be seamless. We noticed many websites cater to either Traditional or Simplified Chinese users, but rarely both with ease.Polyglot Converter 誕生於一個簡單的想法：溝通應該是無縫的。我們注意到許多網站只服務繁體或簡體中文用戶，很少能輕鬆地同時服務兩者。

This lightweight, open-source plugin is our contribution to a more connected web. It's built with performance and simplicity at its core, ensuring anyone can bridge the language gap without technical hurdles.這個輕巧的開源插件是我們對建立更互聯的網路所做的貢獻。它以性能和簡潔為核心，確保任何人都能在沒有技術障礙的情況下跨越語言鴻溝。
